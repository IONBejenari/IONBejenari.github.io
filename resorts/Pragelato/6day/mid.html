    Visitor Reviews of Pragelato (Via Lattea)

    • Vote
    • dave talbot from United Kingdom writes:

      Skiing/boarding at Pragelato (Via Lattea)is pretty good. Groomers left some powder on some pistes for the boarders but there is plenty of off-piste through trees etc. Good fast runs for down-hillers too with minimal waiting at lifts in the week! Food at the hotel was only ok; they cater for vegetarians under duress and the kitchens in the suites, which are great to look at, don't all work, so no coffee making and none served after dinner. The spa can be used for 35 Euros for the week, which the crystal rep didn't tell us even though we asked. You have to find everything out for yourselves and be pushy as some of the staff are really good, but some just can't be bothered! And btw, the football at Inter Milan takes 3-4 hours to get to, not 2 hours like the rep said! Overall rating 60%.
