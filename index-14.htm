PHILOSOPHY

With several years of Building Biology under her belt, following a career in fashion that took her to Rome, Paris, Milan, and London, with stints at Valentino, Ralph Lauren, and British Vogue, Lucie McCullough brings non-toxic homes and interiors to clients whose desire is to encompass HEALTH and DESIGN into their living experience.

‘Building Biology’ or ‘Bau Biologie’ redefines ‘GREEN’ or ‘ECO’. The concept of ‘GREEN’ is environmentally conscious, but Building Biology goes beyond. The health of the occupant and our planet are equally cared for and imagined within our designs.
