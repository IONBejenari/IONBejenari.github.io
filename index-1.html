THE PROPERTY
  • 17th century Piedmontese farmhouse lovingly restored by acclaimed interior designer.
  • Property featured in Architectural Digest (AD)  - the crème de la crème of international interior magazines.
  • Sleeps 12 adults and 6 kids in 5 master ensuites, a media room with a double bed and a kids’ dorm which sleeps 6.
  • Fully furnished to luxurious standard. Bedrooms have custom made egyptian cotton bed sheets and bathrooms have underfloor heating, top of the range fittings and salon standard hair dryers.
  • Drying room with underfloor heating for storage of all ski gear and adjacent laundry room.
  • International film/music library, WiFi & iPod docking stations throughout.
  • Extremely spacious communal living space with wood-burning open fire.
  • Media room with wood-burning open fire (convertible to double bedroom).
  • Mountain-view roof terrace with 8-person wooden hot tub.
  • Lodge within 3 minutes stroll of:
    • cable car to 400km of downhill Olympic piste, known as 'The Milky Way’  (with a downhill return to the lodge door)
    • heli-ski launch pad
    • chair lift to local best-kept secret: 50km of pristine downhill skiing
    • Olympic cross-country piste in renowned Val Troncea National park.
  • Ski rental & English-speaking ski instructor (offering kids pick-up service) within 2 minutes walk
  • Some excellent restaurants in the village, within walking distance, featuring Italian-French fusion, Fondue/Raclette, Cafe, and “Italy’s best pizza restaurant" - winner 2008.
  • 90km from Turin (direct flights from London) and 220 km from Milan (direct international flights, including New York, London and Hong Kong).
